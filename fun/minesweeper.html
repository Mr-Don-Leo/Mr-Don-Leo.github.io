How to Play

The grid hides a set number of mines. Your goal is to reveal every safe cell without clicking a mine.

Left-click (or tap) to reveal a cell. Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to place / remove a 🚩 flag on a suspected mine.

Revealed cells show a number — the count of mines touching that cell. Use these numbers to deduce where the mines are.

Chord-click: if a revealed number already has the right number of flags around it, clicking it will auto-reveal all remaining adjacent cells. Useful once you're confident.

First click is always safe. Mines are placed after your first move.

The timer starts on your first click. Best times are saved per difficulty.
